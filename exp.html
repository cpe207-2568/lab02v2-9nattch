Thailand Greenmech Contest 2023

Participating in the Thailand GreenMech Contest was one of the most exciting and challenging experiences I've had. The competition focused on creating mechanical systems that work without electricity, using only green energy and mechanical principles. Our team had to build a working model using limited materials, and we had to rely on creative thinking, engineering skills, and teamwork.
